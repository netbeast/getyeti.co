Yeti Smart Home is here

Luis Pinto, Head of Content
@luisfpinto_

Yeti Smart Home will lead you to forget about going app by app to control your lights, sound systems and every smart device you have. Create routines to turn on/off your lights when you wake up in the morning. Yeti creates smarter homes

Control all your smart home devices with just one app

Nowadays people are getting smart home devices. They can have Philips Hue, Lifx or Belkin WeMo as a lighting system, Sonos as the sound system, WeMo to control switches, Netatmo for security cameras or heating systems...among others. We find there are many brands on the smart home ecosystem.

Every smart device has a smartphone application which can control the device. For example, if you have Philips Hue lights you’ll need the philips hue application to control them.

If you have more than one brand at home, you have to install not only the Philips Hue app but you also  have to install one app for every brand you have at home to control your devices.

Moreover, you can only control one brand per application. You would have to switch apps every time you want to control more than one device. This sounds ridiculous, doesn’t it?

Yeti Smart Home is an application where you can control all your smarthome devices regardless of their brand. So, for example,  you can control all the lights you have at home even if they are different brands with the same application and you could also control your sound and heating system with that same app.

Today the most you can get are smart devices. Luckily smart homes are on the way.

Fast devices control

Yeti Smart Home has a really easy and intuitive interface where you just need to tap and hold on widgets to quickly control your devices. You could easily control your lights and speakers with only a couple taps on the home screen.

Rooms

You can now control devices all together as a group if they are the same kind of device. For example, if you have two or more lights, automatically an “all bulbs” group will be created so you can perform an action on the widget and it will affect all your lights.
