Yeti is now compatible with Nest Thermostats

A few words about some of the Nest Learning Thermostat's main features.

Today we would like to tell you more about the features of the Nest Learning Thermostat. The product is pretty neat, as it allows you to control the home temperature with your smartphone and at the same time helps you to save money using ECO mode. Say goodbye to wandering in the middle of the night, looking for a thermostat to cool off the room temperature - Let your smart companion Yeti, do the work!

Before going further we need to know how we can integrate Nest into the Yeti app.

How to integrate Nest into Yeti

1. Add nest as a configured brand

‍

To do so you need to click on the + button in the home screen and select "Configure brands". Then tap in the Nest icon among all the brands you are able to select.

‍

‍

2. Connect your Nest account to Yeti

After that, follow all the instructions in the configuration set up, you'll be asked to introduce your nest credential. Finally, you'll be able to control your Nest thermostats using Yeti

‍

‍

What can we do with Nest & Yeti working together?

The 3rd gen Nest Learning Thermostat is a smart device that learns your schedules and helps you save energy. Recently, the thermostat received an Energy Star from EPA, which is an international standard for energy efficient consumer products. According to Nest data, the Thermostat enables saving an average of 10-12% on heating bills and 15% on cooling bills. 

What other features does it offer? Let’s see!

1. Control the home temperature with your mobile app

I am sure you know that moment, when you are in your bed, perfectly cozy, but you suddenly feel cold and the blanket is too far. You can either get up which seems like the hardest thing to do at the time or feel cold. Say “no more” - With Yeti you can control the temperature from any place at your home. You can check the current setup and change it into desired one.

‍

2. Turn on / turn off the thermostat remotely

One of the key benefits of the Nest Thermostat is that it can be controlled remotely. If you forgot to turn off your thermostat, before you left home, you can do it using the app. You are also able to program any temperature you want in advance while you’re at work, school or doing groceries.

Nest Learning Thermostat will rock your home with Yeti

3. Save energy and money using Eco Mode

Nest Thermostat offers you the possibility to cut off some of your electricity bills by the use of Eco-mode. All you need to do is follow the Leaf. You can turn it on manually or let the Home/Away Assist, do the work. With the use of built-in”, Nest by itself figures out if you’re in or out of the house. When you are out, it will change itself into energy-saving mode, so every time you will be gone, you’ll save money. Upon your arrival, the Thermostat will return to your regular schedule. To keep up with all the savings you have made, Nest sends you an email with the monthly home report, so you can get the summary of your energy use. Pretty awesome, isn’t it?

‍

‍

4. Let Nest learn your schedule

The Nest learning Thermostat is a really smart one, as it learns your daily routines and adapts to them. Just show your habits (like turning the temperature down, when your housemates come home or turning it up in the morning when you wake up) and set the preferable temperatures for a week. Auto-Schedule will quickly learn your preferences and add them to your daily plan so you will not have to program them every day.

As you can see the Nest Thermostat is a really awesome smart device. What makes it even cooler, is the fact that now you can include it into your Yeti rooms, routines and charms, making your home even smarter.
