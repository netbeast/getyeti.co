Our Mission

We're on a journey to empower people's homes to be smarter and take care of them & their loved ones

We believe

We're all in this together

In order to truly change the way our home's take care of us for the better we need to get all your devices working together in harmony. To do so, we must forgo any selfish approach and work together with all brands to create the best experience possible for you.

We're here to take care of you

Although technology may be the backbone of all that we do for you, we firmly believe that it's through great caring and support that makes us shine. That's the very reason why we are always available to help you out via our chat inside Yeti!

In designing for ease of use and engineering for perfection

We know that you don't want any extra hassles when it comes to your daily life. That's why we focus on designing smart home control experiences that are simply beautiful and easy to use. To achieve that, we rely on the best technology and engineering expertise working behind the scenes to let your home handle all the work, almost like magic!

Innovation comes best through playfulness

We don't believe in traditional company cultures of meetings and spreadsheets. It just doesn't fit with us. We prefer spending our time talking to you to figure out the best ways to make your life easier then going back to the Yeti cave to our whiteboards to design and build them. We're always open to new ideas and love to try new things out!
